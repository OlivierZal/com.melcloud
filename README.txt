This app integrates MELCloud and MELCloud Home into Homey to control:

MELCloud (classic):
- air-to-air heat pumps;
- air-to-water heat pumps;
- energy recovery ventilators (ERV).

MELCloud Home:
- air-to-air heat pumps.
